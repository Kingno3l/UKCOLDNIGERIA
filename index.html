About UK Cold Nigeria

Welcome to UK COLD NIGERIA LTD!

UK COLD NIGERIA LTD is a purpose driven company committed to mitigating post-harvest losses in Nigeria through clean energy and assisting governments in Africa access climate financing. Our goal is to enable access to global climate funds and promote sustainable development practices in agriculture and related sectors.

Our mission is twofold. Firstly, we aim to reduce post-harvest losses which is over 60%, by providing state-of-the-art off-grid cold chain solutions and expertise. This translates to reducing food waste ,increasing farmers revenue and reducing carbon emissions in the agricultural sector.

Secondly, we are dedicated to assisting governments in Africa to develop brilliant climate finance strategies. We understand the importance of access to global climate funds as an essential tool to enable African economies transition to carbon neutral. Thus we work closely with governments, organisations, and stakeholders to reach eligibility and unlock financing opportunities for sustainable climate change mitigation and adaptation initiatives in agriculture an other key sectors.

We envision a Nigeria where post-harvest losses are significantly minimised, thus improving food availability and affordability, ensuring food security and promoting economic growth. Additionally, we aspire to be a global leader in facilitating climate finance strategies, assisting governments in developing nations access funds and implement sustainable projects that combat climate change.
